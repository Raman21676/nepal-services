Frequently Asked Questions (Voice Search Optimized)

What is the emergency ambulance number in Nepal?

The emergency ambulance number in Nepal is 102. You can also call 100 for police, 101 for fire services, and 103 for traffic police. Nepal Services Directory provides a comprehensive list of ambulance services and emergency contacts across all 7 provinces.

How do I find the best hospital near me in Kathmandu?

Use Nepal Services Directory to find the best hospitals near you in Kathmandu. Search by your location, filter by specialty (cardiac, maternity, pediatric), and view ratings, contact numbers, and addresses. Top hospitals include Grande International Hospital, Norvic International Hospital, Teaching Hospital, and Bir Hospital.

Where can I find schools in Pokhara?

Nepal Services Directory lists all schools in Pokhara and Gandaki Province. Filter by school type (government, private, boarding), educational level, and programs offered. You can find contact information, addresses, and details for schools in Pokhara city and surrounding areas.

Is Nepal Services Directory free to use?

Yes, Nepal Services Directory is completely free to use. We provide access to over 5,300 verified services including hospitals, schools, emergency contacts, and government offices across all 77 districts of Nepal. No registration or payment is required.

How accurate is the information on Nepal Services Directory?

Our data is sourced from official government websites, verified hospital records, and educational institution databases. Emergency contact numbers are verified monthly, and facility information is updated quarterly. However, we recommend calling ahead to confirm details before visiting.
