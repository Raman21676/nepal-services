Why Healthcare in Pokhara Matters

Pokhara serves as the primary healthcare destination for over 2 million people living in Gandaki Province. With thousands of international tourists and trekkers visiting each year, the city's hospitals have invested heavily in modern equipment, English-speaking staff, and specialized services. From emergency trauma care to specialized surgeries, hospitals in Pokhara now rival those in Kathmandu for many treatments.

The city's medical infrastructure has improved dramatically over the past decade. Major hospitals now offer CT scans, MRI services, dialysis, cardiac care, and advanced surgical procedures. For anyone living in or visiting Pokhara, understanding your healthcare options is essential.

🏆 Top Hospitals in Pokhara

1. Manipal Teaching Hospital Pokhara

When people search for the best hospital in Pokhara, Manipal Teaching Hospital consistently tops the list. Established as part of the Manipal College of Medical Sciences, this 700-bed tertiary care hospital is the largest and most advanced medical facility in Western Nepal.

Manipal Hospital Pokhara - Key Information

  • Location: Phulbari, Pokhara-17, Kaski
  • Bed Capacity: 700+ beds
  • Emergency: 24/7 trauma and emergency services
  • Specialties: Cardiology, Neurology, Orthopedics, Oncology, Nephrology, General Surgery
  • Contact: 061-526416, 061-526417
  • Website: manipalpokhara.edu.np

Manipal Hospital Pokhara houses state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment including CT scan, MRI, digital X-ray, and a fully automated laboratory. The hospital's ICU and NICU are among the best equipped in the region. With experienced consultants from Nepal and India, patients receive world-class treatment at a fraction of international costs. The hospital also runs a medical college, ensuring a steady supply of trained healthcare professionals.

2. Fishtail Hospital Pokhara

Fishtail Hospital is another premier private hospital in Pokhara known for its patient-centered care and modern facilities. Located conveniently in the heart of the city, Fishtail Hospital has earned a reputation for excellence in general surgery, maternity services, and pediatric care.

Fishtail Hospital - Key Information

  • Location: Srijana Chowk, Pokhara
  • Bed Capacity: 150+ beds
  • Emergency: 24/7 emergency department
  • Specialties: General Surgery, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Pediatrics, Internal Medicine, Orthopedics
  • Contact: 061-537301

Fishtail Hospital's maternity ward is particularly popular among families in Pokhara, offering safe delivery packages and neonatal care. The hospital's clean environment, attentive nursing staff, and affordable pricing make it a strong competitor to larger institutions.

3. Charak Memorial Hospital

Charak Memorial Hospital is a trusted name in Pokhara's healthcare landscape. This multi-specialty hospital provides comprehensive medical services ranging from routine checkups to complex surgeries. Charak Memorial is known for its experienced doctors and reliable diagnostic services.

Charak Memorial Hospital - Key Information

  • Location: Chipledhunga, Pokhara
  • Emergency: 24/7
  • Specialties: General Medicine, Surgery, ENT, Ophthalmology, Dermatology
  • Contact: 061-520800

4. Om Hospital & Research Centre

Om Hospital has expanded its presence to Pokhara, bringing its legacy of quality healthcare from Kathmandu. The Pokhara branch offers advanced laparoscopic surgery, urology services, and comprehensive health checkup packages. Many patients prefer Om Hospital for its standardized protocols and transparent billing.

5. Pokhara Academy of Health Sciences (PAHS)

PAHS is a government-backed institution that plays a dual role as a teaching hospital and public healthcare provider. While primarily serving as a training center for medical students, PAHS hospital provides affordable treatment options for common ailments, maternal health, and primary care services. It's an excellent choice for budget-conscious patients seeking reliable care.

🚑 Emergency Medical Services in Pokhara

Medical emergencies can happen anytime, especially with Pokhara's adventure tourism activities like trekking, paragliding, and rafting. All major hospitals in Pokhara provide 24/7 emergency services, but Manipal Teaching Hospital has the most comprehensive trauma center equipped to handle critical injuries.

For immediate ambulance services in Pokhara, you can call:

  • National Ambulance: 102
  • Red Cross Ambulance: 1130
  • Manipal Hospital Emergency: 061-526416

💡 Tips for Choosing a Hospital in Pokhara

  1. Check specialties: For cardiac or neurological issues, Manipal Hospital is your best bet. For maternity, consider Fishtail Hospital.
  2. Verify insurance: Call ahead to confirm if your health insurance is accepted.
  3. Location matters: In emergencies, choose the nearest hospital with emergency facilities.
  4. Doctor availability: Major hospitals have specialist doctors on rotation; check schedules for non-emergency visits.
  5. Cost comparison: Private hospitals vary significantly in pricing. Government facilities like PAHS offer lower costs.
