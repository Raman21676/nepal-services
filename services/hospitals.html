❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Hospitals in Nepal

How many hospitals are there in Nepal?

There are over 500 hospitals in Nepal including government district hospitals, private hospitals, medical colleges, and teaching hospitals across all 7 provinces.

Which is the best hospital in Kathmandu?

Tribhuvan University Teaching Hospital (TUTH) and Bir Hospital are among the top government hospitals. For private care, Norvic International Hospital and Grande International Hospital are highly rated.

Are hospitals in Nepal affordable?

Government hospitals offer subsidized rates and free services for certain categories. Private hospitals vary in pricing but are generally more affordable than Western countries.

Do Nepal hospitals have emergency services?

Yes, all major district hospitals and private hospitals provide 24/7 emergency services. Teaching hospitals have dedicated emergency departments.
