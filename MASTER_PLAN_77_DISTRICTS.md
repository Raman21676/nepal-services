# Nepal Services Directory - Master Plan for All 77 Districts

## Coverage Target
**All 77 districts of Nepal** with hospitals, clinics, emergency services, and government offices.

---

## Data Collection Standards

### Mandatory Fields for Each District
1. **Hospitals** (Government + Private)
   - Name, address, phone, type, beds, emergency availability
2. **Clinics** (Polyclinics, Specialized)
   - Name, address, phone, services, doctors (with NMC if available)
3. **Emergency Services**
   - Ambulance, Police, Fire, Blood Bank
4. **Ward Offices** (for municipalities)
   - Ward number, secretary contact, address
5. **District Administration**
   - CDO office, DHO office, District Hospital

### Legal Compliance (Already Verified)
- ✅ NMC numbers: SAFE (public registry)
- ✅ Office phone numbers: SAFE
- ✅ Doctor names (professional): SAFE
- ❌ Personal mobiles: NOT collected
- ❌ Home addresses: NOT collected
